The Problem

In this age of digital tech, cameras are everywhere. Taking a picture is so easy now but the real issue is how to share these photos in a convenient and efficient way. As an avid traveller, I love going on trips and taking photos. But, at the end of every trip, I spend hours uploading my images, sorting out which image goes to who, sharing them with others, and downloading other people’s pictures. As a personal project with friends, I decided to create an app that provides a fast and convenient way for users to upload, share, and download images while also improving my technical skills.

Meet Keith

Keith has worked as a biotech researcher for many years. He’s never gotten around to using social media apps and prefers more face to face contact with others. His recent company retreat has given him several memories that he wishes to look back on, but his colleagues aren’t avid phone users either.

Meet Emily

Emily just started college and ended up taking many pictures with her family during her spring break. Soon, Emily and her siblings will be rushing back to their own busy lives, thus Emily wants to easily exchange photos with them before they go.
